CERTIFICATE

Academic Document signed by the Vice Chancellor and the Registrar confirming the completion of an academic programme

Requirements For CERTIFICATE

  • Sworn Affidavit Stating Intention to Collect Certificate via Courier Delivery
  • Copy of Valid means of Identification (i.e. International Passport, National ID Card, Voter's Card or Driver's License)
  • Copy of Marriage Certificate or Newspaper Publication on Change of Name (Applicable ONLY to married women who have changed their maiden name)
  • White Background Passport size Photograph (jpeg or jpg format only and it should be renamed using full name before upload)
  • Notification of Result
  • School Fees Receipt or Evidence of Payment of Five Thousand Naira (₦ 5,000) for Loss of Receipt or Original Student's Statement of Account
  • Evidence of payment of One Thousand Naira (₦ 1,000) only Alumni Due
